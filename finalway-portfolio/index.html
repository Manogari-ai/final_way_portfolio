How a
project runs

The same four-stage pipeline whether it's a portrait session or a three-day shoot.

Stage 1

Brief & Scout

We define the look, the deliverables, and the locations before a single frame is shot.

Stage 2

Shoot Day

On-set direction, lighting, and camera operation — solo or with a small crew depending on scope.

Stage 3

Edit & Grade

Selects, cut, color, and sound pass. You see a draft before anything is locked.

Stage 4

Deliver

Final files exported to spec — web, broadcast, or print — with source files archived.
